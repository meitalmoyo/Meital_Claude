# LinkedIn Content — ch01: Profile Strategy

Positioning Meital on LinkedIn as a mediator-in-training with a design background.

---

## The Positioning Challenge

Meital is in transition: experienced designer + emerging mediator. The LinkedIn profile must:
- Not confuse people about what she does
- Use the design background as an asset, not a distraction
- Build credibility as a future mediator now, before certification

**Core positioning:** A mediator-in-training with rare visual communication skills. The design background isn't separate — it makes her a mediator who can communicate complex processes clearly, create materials clients actually understand, and build a brand that projects trust.

---

## Headline (כותרת)

The headline is the most visible element after the name. It appears in search results, comments, and connection requests.

**Formula:** [Who you are] | [What you help with] | [Differentiator]

**Current recommendation:**
`מגשרת (בתהליך הכשרה) | מעצבת ויזואלית | MOYO Productions`

Or more benefit-focused:
`עוזרת לאנשים לפתור סכסוכים בדרך הנכונה | גישור | עיצוב`

**Rules:**
- Hebrew first (Israeli audience is primary)
- Include "גישור" (mediation) — it's the searchable keyword
- Don't hide the "in training" status — it's honest and actually endearing
- 120 characters max (LinkedIn limit)

---

## About Section (אודות)

First 2–3 lines are visible without clicking "see more" — make them count.

**Structure:**
1. **Opening hook** — who you are, what you care about (not your job title)
2. **The story** — why mediation? what drives you?
3. **What you offer** — services, approach, values
4. **Call to action** — how to reach you

**Tone:** Warm, personal, first-person. This is not a CV summary. It's a conversation opener.

**Draft starting point:**
> אני מאמינה שרוב הסכסוכים לא צריכים להגיע לבית משפט.
> [Continue with personal story of why mediation...]
> כיום אני בתהליך הכשרה כמגשרת ב[Bar Ilan], לצד עשור של עבודה כמעצבת ויזואלית.
> [What the combination means for clients...]
