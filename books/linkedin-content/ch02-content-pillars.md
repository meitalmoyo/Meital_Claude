# LinkedIn Content — ch02: Content Pillars

What to post about. Organized around the mediator brand for Israeli audience.

---

## The 4 Pillars

### Pillar 1: Education — "לא ידעתי ש..."
Teach the audience something they didn't know about conflict, mediation, or communication.

**Why it works:** Positions you as an expert. People share useful information. Builds trust before you ask for anything.

**Topics:**
- What mediation actually is (and isn't — not a judge, not a therapist)
- When mediation is better than a lawyer
- Common myths about conflict
- The difference between positions and interests (explained simply)
- What happens in a mediation session
- Types of conflict that can be mediated (family, workplace, neighbor disputes)
- Statistics about mediation outcomes in Israel

**Format:** Short insight + practical takeaway. "Many people think X. Actually Y. What this means for you: Z."

---

### Pillar 2: Personal Story — "מה שלמדתי מ..."
Share personal experiences, lessons, and the journey.

**Why it works:** People connect with people, not professionals. Vulnerability builds trust.

**Topics:**
- Why I chose mediation (the personal story)
- What studying conflict management taught me about myself
- A moment in my design career where I needed mediation skills
- What I'm learning this semester at Bar Ilan
- A difficult conversation I had and what I did differently

**Format:** Story arc — situation → struggle → lesson. End with a question or reflection for the reader.

---

### Pillar 3: Practical Tips — "איך לעשות את זה"
Actionable communication and conflict skills the audience can use immediately.

**Why it works:** High shareability. People save and send practical content.

**Topics:**
- How to give difficult feedback without damaging the relationship
- 3 questions to ask before escalating a conflict
- How to say "no" without burning bridges
- What to do when a conversation gets heated
- The one thing to remember in any difficult negotiation
- How to know if a conflict is solvable or not

**Format:** Numbered list or "one simple thing you can try today." Practical, not theoretical.

---

### Pillar 4: Perspective — "מה שאני חושבת על..."
Opinion, observation, or take on something in the world through a conflict/communication lens.

**Why it works:** Differentiates your voice. Sparks conversation.

**Topics:**
- A news story with a mediation angle
- Why Israeli culture makes some conflicts harder to resolve (and some easier)
- What workplaces get wrong about conflict
- The problem with "winning" an argument
- Why apologies are so hard (and so powerful)

**Format:** Clear point of view + reasoning + question to the reader. Not a rant — a considered perspective.

---

## Content Mix (Suggested)

| Pillar | Frequency |
|--------|---------|
| Education | 1× per 2 weeks |
| Personal Story | 1× per 2 weeks |
| Practical Tips | 1× per week |
| Perspective | 1× per 2 weeks |

**Starting out:** Post at least once a week. Consistency beats frequency for LinkedIn algorithm.

---

## Topics to Avoid

- Generic motivational quotes (not distinctive, low value)
- Overtly political conflict (risks alienating audience)
- Anything confidential from studies or practice
- Excessive self-promotion without value ("I'm available for mediation" with nothing else)
