## Patterns (Review Monthly)

After 4–6 posts, review:
- Which pillar gets the most comments?
- Which format gets the most shares?
- What topics spark the most conversation?
- What time of day did the highest-performing posts go out?

Use these patterns to refine the content calendar — keep what works, drop what doesn't.
