## Core Services

### גישור משפחתי / Family Mediation
**Who it's for:** Couples separating, parents in custody disputes, family inheritance conflicts, sibling disagreements

**What it is:** A confidential process guided by a neutral mediator to help family members reach their own agreements — without court, without adversarial lawyers, and with the family relationship as a priority.

**What they gain:** More control over the outcome, less cost than litigation, faster resolution, preserved relationships where possible.

**Draft tagline:** "הסכם שאתם בחרתם — לא שאחרים כפו עליכם"

---

### גישור עסקי / Commercial Mediation
**Who it's for:** Business partners in dispute, client-supplier conflicts, workplace disagreements, contractor disputes

**What it is:** Structured, confidential mediation for business disputes. Faster and less expensive than litigation. Agreements are binding when signed.

**What they gain:** Speed (days or weeks vs. months or years), confidentiality (unlike court proceedings), preserved business relationships.

**Draft tagline:** "פתרון מהיר, חסוי, ועם פחות נזק לעסק"

---

### גישור שכנים / Neighbor Mediation
**Who it's for:** Disputes over noise, property boundaries, shared spaces, parking, building management

**What it is:** A less formal mediation process that helps neighbors reach workable agreements and preserve day-to-day coexistence.

**Draft tagline:** "כי אתם עדיין צריכים לחיות ליד זה"

2. **Genuine warmth** — her background in design client work has trained her to listen, to manage difficult conversations, and to find solutions that work for people, not just on paper
