# Bar Ilan Academic Conventions

Writing standards for Master's assignments in Conflict Management & Negotiation.

---

## General Format

- **Language:** Hebrew (unless assignment specifies English)
- **Font:** David or Arial, 12pt (confirm per course)
- **Line spacing:** 1.5 or double (confirm per course)
- **Margins:** Standard (2.5cm all sides typical)
- **Submission:** Usually via Moodle (Bar Ilan's LMS)

---

## Academic Hebrew Register

**Tone:** Formal, scholarly — not conversational
**Verb form:** Third person or passive where possible ("ניתן לטעון כי..." rather than "אני טוען...")
**Hedging:** Academic claims are qualified — "לפי [theorist]", "ניתן לראות כי", "המחקר מצביע על"
**Avoid:** Slang, first-person in analytical sections, emotional language in analysis

---

## Structure (Standard Academic Paper)

1. **מבוא (Introduction)**
   - Define the question or topic
   - Explain the structure of the paper
   - State the argument or thesis

2. **סקירת ספרות (Literature Review)**
   - Survey relevant theories and scholars
   - Show familiarity with the academic conversation
   - Note agreements and tensions in the literature

3. **ניתוח (Analysis)**
   - Apply theory to the case or question
   - Build the argument systematically
   - Address counterarguments

4. **דיון ומסקנות (Discussion & Conclusions)**
   - Summarize findings
   - Implications
   - Limitations and open questions

5. **ביבליוגרפיה (Bibliography)**

---

## Citation Style

Bar Ilan typically uses **APA** style. Confirm with each course syllabus.

**APA in-text (Hebrew):** (שם משפחה, שנה)
Example: (פישר ואורי, 1991)

**APA reference list (book):**
שם משפחה, אות שם פרטי. (שנה). *שם הספר*. הוצאה לאור.

**APA reference list (article):**
שם משפחה, אות שם פרטי. (שנה). כותרת המאמר. *שם כתב העת*, כרך(גיליון), עמ'–עמ'.

## Key Academic Verbs (Hebrew)

| To express... | Use... |
|--------------|--------|
| Claiming | טוען, גורס, מציין |
| Analyzing | מנתח, בוחן, סוקר |
| Suggesting | מציע, מעלה את הטענה ש |
| Contrasting | לעומת זאת, בניגוד ל |
| Concluding | לסיכום, לאור האמור לעיל |
| Citing | על פי, לדברי, כפי ש[X] מציין |

## Common Assignment Types

**ניתוח מקרה (Case Analysis)**
- Describe the conflict
- Apply a theoretical framework
- Analyze what happened and why
- Suggest alternative outcomes or interventions

**מסה תיאורטית (Theoretical Essay)**
- Engage with a specific theory or theorist
- Compare perspectives
- Build an original argument

**עבודת סמינר (Seminar Paper)**
- Longer, more independent research
- Usually includes original analysis or a research question
- More extensive bibliography expected
