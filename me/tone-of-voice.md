# Tone of Voice

## Default Communication Style
- Casual and direct
- Mix of Hebrew and English is natural and welcome
- Practical over theoretical — get to the point
- Not overly formal

## Context-Specific Registers

### Mediator / Professional (LinkedIn, website)
- Warm, empathetic, trustworthy
- Professional but human
- Hebrew primary audience (Israeli market)

### Design / Client Work
- Creative, confident
- Visual language — paint a picture
- Proposal tone: clear value, not overselling

### University Assignments
- Academic tone
- Structured arguments
- Hebrew (Bar Ilan)
